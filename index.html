Get on Google Play Get on the App Store

What it does

codes. helps small businesses, marketers, and partner networks distribute single-use promotional codes and vouchers without spreadsheets or messy WhatsApp threads.

Codes & vouchers

Track promo codes (one-time use) and vouchers (issue and redeem) in separate categories. Bulk import via CSV or paste-from-clipboard.

Team mode

Pair up to 2 phones securely. The Owner controls the inventory; Distributors hand out codes in the field. End-to-end encrypted.

Cloud sync (Online tier)

Optional silent sync between paired phones via an encrypted relay. The relay never sees your codes — only encrypted bytes.

Multi-page voucher PDFs

Generate printable voucher booklets with QR codes for redemption. Print-ready for local print shops.

Offline-first

Works without internet. Everything lives on your phone. Cloud sync is opt-in.

No accounts

No sign-up. No email collection. No tracking. Just install and use.

Pricing

Free tier: up to 200 active codes; single-phone use.

Pro Team ($9.99 one-time): unlimited codes, team mode, bulk distribution, partner database, PDF vouchers, daily team status reports — file-share transport.

Pro Online ($24.99 one-time): everything in Pro Team, plus silent cloud sync, request-codes flow, and the live team-status panel.

Upgrade ($15.99): if you already own Pro Team and want to add Online sync.

All purchases are one-time. No subscriptions. Distributor phones are free via Owner's invite — the Owner pays once.
