Privacy by design

codes. stores everything locally on your device. There are no analytics, no telemetry, and no accounts. Team transfers between devices are encrypted with standard AES-GCM. By default they travel as files you share yourself; if you opt into Pro Online cloud sync, the same end-to-end-encrypted messages are relayed through our server — which can never read their contents.

External services are kept to a minimum: RevenueCat for in-app purchase receipt validation, and (only if you enable cloud sync) a relay server on Cloudflare that carries encrypted messages it cannot read. Neither ever sees your codes, customers, or any business data. See the privacy policy for details.
