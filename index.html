Privacy by design

codes. stores everything locally on your device. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no accounts, no servers receiving your data. Team transfers between devices are encrypted with standard AES-GCM and never touch any cloud service.

The only external service is RevenueCat, used exclusively for in-app purchase receipt validation. RevenueCat receives purchase events from Apple and Google — it never sees your codes, customers, or any business data.
