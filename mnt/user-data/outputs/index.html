                          What the numbers mean

                          The status line under each category shows live counts.

                          • Available
                            Codes still in your inventory, ready to give out.
                          • Used (promo)
                            Promo codes you've already handed to a customer or recipient.
                          • Issued (voucher)
                            Vouchers you've handed out — printed or shared. The customer might or might not have redeemed them yet.
                          • Redeemed (voucher)
                            Vouchers you scanned back when the customer actually used them. This is a subset of "issued" — once you've redeemed a voucher it's no longer pending.

                          Requests from your team

                          Distributors have asked you to send them codes. Tap Fulfill to open the send sheet pre-filled, or Dismiss to remove the request.

                                        What gets loaded
                                        • 3 promo code categories — iOS, Android, and URL-based — with 100 sample codes each

                                        What you'll see
                                        • A fake teammate "Demo Phone" added to your roster
                                        • The team-status panel populated with their stock
                                        • A pending code request you can practice fulfilling
                                        Read-only — sending real codes to "Demo Phone" won't go anywhere. Tap "Exit demo" anytime to wipe it.
                                        ⚠ Demo only works on a fresh install. If you've added real codes or set up a real team, please clear that first.

                                        Preferred app for team transfers

                                        Pick how codes. sends files between team members — transfer packages, confirmations, status reports. Direct-app shortcuts skip the share chooser. If the chosen app isn't installed, codes. falls back to the system share sheet.

                                        Customer-facing shares (codes you send to recipients, voucher images) always use the full system share sheet, so you can pick any channel — SMS, email, WhatsApp, print, etc.

                                          Note: Your team includes iOS members. Direct-app shortcuts are skipped for iOS distributors automatically; the system share sheet is used instead for cross-platform reliability.

                                          Language

                                          Choose your preferred language. The app will fall back to English for any text that hasn't been translated yet.
