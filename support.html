Frequently asked questions

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. The free tier and Pro Team tier are entirely offline. The only feature that needs internet is the optional cloud sync in Pro Online — and even that automatically falls back to file-share mode if there's no connection.

How do I move my data to a new phone?

Settings → Export creates a JSON backup of all your codes, categories, customers, and team configuration. Transfer the file to your new phone (email, AirDrop, USB), then Settings → Import.

Backups are not encrypted by default. Store them somewhere safe and delete after import if they contain sensitive data.

Can I un-pair from a team?

Yes. Settings → Team → "Leave team" (Distributor) or "Disband team" (Owner). On Owner disband, all Distributors receive a disconnect message and their team-tagged data becomes read-only.

What happens to in-flight cloud messages if I disable sync?

Any messages already addressed to your phone will be delivered the next time you re-enable sync, until they expire (currently 7 days). Pending outbound messages on your side will fall back to file-share when you next try to send.

Why am I seeing a "version mismatch" banner?

A paired phone in your team is running a newer version of the app. Update yours from the App Store / Google Play to get full compatibility. The banner is dismissible if you'd prefer to wait.

How do I clear practice ("demo") data?

Settings → Load practice data → "Clear practice data" button (only visible after demo data has been loaded). Or for the team-mode preview: tap "Exit demo" on the banner at the top of the Codes view.

I bought Pro on my old phone — how do I restore it?

Settings → Pro features → "Restore purchase". You'll need to be signed in to the same Apple ID or Google account that made the original purchase.

Can I refund a Pro purchase?

Refunds are handled by the platform (Apple App Store or Google Play) under their own policies — we can't refund directly. See:

  • Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google: Refund process

Is my data backed up automatically?

No. The app does not auto-back-up to any cloud. We recommend running Settings → Export periodically and storing the backup somewhere safe (email-to-self, Drive, iCloud, etc.).

⚠ If your phone is lost or its storage is wiped, all data is gone. Cloud sync (Online tier) is not a backup — it routes messages, not snapshots.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Email support@evdiag.net with as much detail as you can. Screenshots help a lot.
